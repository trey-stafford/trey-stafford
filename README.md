### Hello! 👋


I am a software engineer and supervisor at the [National Snow and Ice Data
Center (NSIDC)](https://nsidc.org/home). I have a background in [Geographic
Information Systems
(GIS)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geographic_information_system) and
geospatial data formats, and I am the co-creator of the software that produces
[QGreenland](https://qgreenland.org). I also act as lead developer for
[NOAA@NSIDC](https://nsidc.org/data/data-programs/noaa-nsidc) sea ice products
(e.g., the [Sea Ice Index](https://nsidc.org/data/seaice_index)) and I provide
scientific data reviews for one of the [NASA NSIDC
DAAC](https://nsidc.org/data/data-programs/nsidc-daac)'s data publication
teams. During my tenure at NSIDC, I have worked with teams composed of
scientists, projects and programs leads, data operations specialists, and
systems administrators. In these cross-functional teams, I help to design and
implement full-stack software architectures for science data processing,
publication and archival, and analysis and visualization.

Topics of interest to me include:

* Building tools with Python to facilitate understanding of complex science data
* Geographic Information Systems (GIS)
* Remote Sensing
* Open Source / Open Science
* Reproducibility in science
* Earth science (especially about the cryosphere!)
